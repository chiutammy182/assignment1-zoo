Koala

The koala is a herbivore native to Australia. As of last year, koalas are considered endangered. It is estimated that there are less than 100,000 koalas remaining in eastern Australia. Because of koalas' low energy diet and a great amount of energy needed to break down toxic leaves, koalas sleep for 20 hours a day. Additionally, they are considered noctural as they are mostly active at night, dawn and dusk. Their life span in the wild is around 13 to 18 years. This species also prefers to spend time alone.
